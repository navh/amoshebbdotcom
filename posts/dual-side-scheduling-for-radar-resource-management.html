Dual Side Scheduling for Radar Resource Management

2023-01-28

Bottom Line Up Front

Acronyms

  • MFR: multi-function radar
  • RRM: radar resource management
  • EST: earliest-start-time (scheduling method)
  • ED: earliest-deadline (secheduling method)
  • DSS: dual side scheduling
  • NCT: nearest closer time
  • RSST: Random shifted start time

Summary

Instead of jamming tasks left, toss out some random points and scrunch them together about them. Fast enough to calculate, drop fewer and cost’s less after checking a bunch for one that costs less.

Intro

Phased array radars have multiple functions, which need different tasks completed.

Tasks are described by priority pp, and times tstartt_{start}, tdwellt_{dwell}, tearliestt_{earliest}, tlatestt_{latest}.

Radar deal with hundreds of tasks in tens of milliseconds.

Cost JJ, and drop rate γ\gamma, schedule solution $t_{sched}, computational time TT to determine the solution.

Scheduling is NP-hard.

Current tradeoff is low TT, high JJ and γ\gamma, or inverse.

Problem Formulation

Radar handles NN tasks in window length LL, with L=1L = 1.

No Dwell interleaving in this paper. Ideal averaged tdwell=1/Nt_{dwell} = 1 / N.

No underloading or overloading of the scheduler. Input tasks equal tasks system is designed to handle.

Total cost is the sum of task costs.

Task cost formulae are given, if a task is scheduled, it costs 0-1, if dropped, 1-9. Dropping low priority task costs more than delaying high priority.

Dual-Side Scheduling

Currently, schedules pack front to back, no gaps. This may shift task from original tstartt_{start}, which should be expensive.

**Proposal, place the separator at location ss, and let tasks on each side shift toward separator. Name: Dual-side scheduling (DSS).

Numerical Simulations

Monte Carlo simulations, 1000 rounds, DSS has a lower drop rate. DSS and RSS drop fewer (unclear to me why this would be the case), RSST costs more at the start, and large numbers become similar. The computational time of DSS is lower. (This confuses me, I don’t understand how random could be worse than exhaustive evaluation, maybe it does more rounds than ss are selected?) EST costs zero.

Conclusion

DSS is computationally fast enough, does better at other things. TODO: pick ss in a smart way.
